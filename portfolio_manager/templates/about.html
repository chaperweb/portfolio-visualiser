PORTFOLIO VISUALIZER

Purpose

This software aims to be a tool for collaborative, open and agile portfolio and other project related data management. The idea is to build a tool which allows opening the information to every member of the organization and possibly also to the customers. The tool helps in creating common understanding and in making decisions by visualizing the data in multiple ways.

Background

Portfolio Visualizer originates from Aalto University School of Science course CS-C2130 - ohjelmistoprojekti 1. This phase of development took place between 14.9.2016 and 19.4.2017.

The original project team constructed of 9 members: Scrum master Nico Liljestrand and 8 developers, Miili Halkka, Joonas Harjumäki, Niklas Heijari, Kaarlo Kekkonen, Iiro Koivulehto, Castor Köhler, Marcos Ryhänen Rodellas and Lauri Voipio. Codentos' representative, as Product Owner, was Matti Kinnunen. Team received mentoring and coaching also from Miika Kuha and Kaj Mustikkamäki from Codento and Benjamin Behm from Agilefant.
